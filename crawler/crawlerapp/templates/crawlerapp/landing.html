Youtube Crawler

A tool building highly customized, mutlimodal datasets for training models


file_download

Download and filter jobs

Once your crawl has finished, click the download button on the job page. We will download your videos to our server, allowing you to filter them using our custom designed filters. You can then add the job to a dataset and download a csv file containing the relevant information, giving you insight into your new dataset.

video_library

Collect videos en masse

On the create job page, you can specify all the parameters YouTube can ask for, giving you full control of the content added to your datasets.

create

Build data sets quickly

Once you have completed a variety of jobs, click on the create dataset page where you can add any combination of your jobs to a dataset. This hierarchical approach allows you to create unique, personalized datasets to train a wide variety of models. Different jobs can be filtered for different parameters, allowing for complex and specialized datasets.
